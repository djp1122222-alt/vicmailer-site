Privacy Policy — Vicmailer

Last updated: July 2026

1. What Data We Collect

When you purchase Vicmailer, we collect:

  • Your email address (from Gumroad)
  • Your name (from Gumroad)
  • Order details (product, price, date)

We do not collect any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to:

  • Deliver your purchase
  • Send you the download link
  • Contact you about your order if needed

We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely via Gumroad (our payment processor). We do not store your data on our own servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties, except:

  • Gumroad (payment processing)
  • Brevo (email delivery, if you contact us)

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at vic@fixseo.uk.

6. Cookies

Vicmailer does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

8. Contact
